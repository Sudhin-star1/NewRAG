Policy and Procedure Statement
The Foundation will use progressive discipline forall employees whose performance requires improvement.The
purpose of the process is to correct, not punish,those employees who have not followed Foundation guiding
principles, policies and procedures. Consistent andfair procedures for progressive development giveemployees
ample opportunity to improve. Definitions:
Progressive discipline:
● Emphasizes correction and establishes clear goalsto help employees change from unsatisfactory
performance to satisfactory performance. ● Focuses on the performance rather than the person. Policies and Procedures Template - Page37
